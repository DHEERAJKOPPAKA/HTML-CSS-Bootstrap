Our Best Recipes

Pasta

Ingredients:
  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp olive oil
Instructions:

Boil pasta, sauté garlic, add sauce, and mix well. Serve hot.

Pizza

Ingredients:
  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup cheese
  • Veggies of choice
Instructions:

Spread sauce on the base, add toppings, and bake at 200°C for 15 min.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
Instructions:

Mix ingredients, bake at 180°C for 30 min. Cool and serve.
